Privacy Policy for Sb1delay

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sb1delay collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our application. Sb1delay is a public analytical tool designed for TikTok creators to monitor and analyze their growth and engagement metrics.

1. Information Collection and Use

Sb1delay collects public TikTok data via authorized API access, including:

  • Follower counts and following counts.
  • Like counts and video statistics.
  • Public profile information (username, bio).

This data is used solely to provide performance insights to the authorized user who connects their account.

2. Data Processing and Storage

Strict Privacy: Sb1delay does not store your TikTok passwords, private messages, or sensitive account information. We do not use external databases to store your profile data. Information is processed in real-time to generate your analytical reports.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Access to the data is provided exclusively to the user who has authorized their TikTok account for their own professional analysis.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect the integrity of the API connection and ensure that data is handled securely during the analytical process.
